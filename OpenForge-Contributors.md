## Why Contribute?

OpenForge is built as an open platform for developers to collaborate on real projects with transparency. Contributions help shape the platform itself and the projects built on top of it.

You can contribute by:

* Improving the OpenForge codebase
* Reporting bugs or UX issues
* Suggesting features
* Participating in projects hosted on OpenForge

## About Tokens & Funding (Important)

* Tokens are **not platform rewards**
* Tokens are **not guaranteed**
* Tokens (if used) belong to the **project**, not OpenForge
* Funding mechanisms are part of **project experimentation**

OpenForge acts as a **collaboration layer**, not a financial intermediary.

---

## Contribution Guidelines

Please follow these principles:

* Be respectful and professional
* Do not overclaim experience or results
* Communicate clearly before starting funded work
* Ask questions if terms are unclear
* Never pressure others for funding

---

## What OpenForge Does NOT Do

To avoid confusion, OpenForge:

* ‚ùå Does not promise tokens for contributions
